Welcome To ONE VAVIYA

ONE VAVIYA is a premium residential development by Neelkanth Developers, located in the well-planned locality of Kharghar. The project is situated at Plot No. 5A, Valley Shilp Road, next to Harmony School in Sector 36, offering excellent connectivity and a peaceful residential environment. Designed for modern urban living, the development combines comfort, convenience, and a prime location in one of Navi Mumbai’s most desirable neighborhoods.

The project offers thoughtfully designed 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and spacious 5 BHK (Jodi) residences with well-planned layouts. The carpet areas include 2 BHK – 768 & 804 sq.ft., 3 BHK – 996 & 1048 sq.ft., and 5 BHK – approximately 1800 sq.ft., providing ample living space for families of different sizes. With its premium construction quality and modern lifestyle offerings, ONE VAVIYA is crafted to deliver a luxurious and comfortable living experience.

About Neelkanth Developers

Neelkanth Developers is a well-known real estate developer primarily operating in Navi Mumbai. Established in 2003, the company has built a strong reputation for developing residential and commercial projects with a focus on quality construction, modern design, and customer satisfaction. Over the years, the group has delivered 30+ projects and created homes for thousands of families across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.

Known for its commitment to reliability and innovation, Neelkanth Developers emphasizes strategic project locations, well-planned layouts, and contemporary amenities. The company aims to enhance urban living by creating sustainable developments that combine comfort, connectivity, and long-term value for homeowners and investors.
